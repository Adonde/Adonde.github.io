My name is Harvvey and I'm a mod on the Mineplex Network.

At the mitst of the Holiday Seaons, I personally will be holding some in-game reward systems via this webpage. These rewards will be tallied up by a simple ticket system, in which for every 10 minutes you spend on any minigame on hypixel, you earn one ticket. All these tickets will be collected. Winners will be posted on this site, and from said drawing date, you will have 48 hours to post a thread on the forums. If failed to comply within 48 hours, a new user will be chosen.
